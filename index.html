Agro Input
Assurance Supply Chain
System

An Intergrated system that assists in monitoring and verification of agro-inputs in the supply chain, from manufacturers to suppliers, retailers and finally to the farmer.

“Its a System that monitors agro inputs in the supply chain .”

As they traverses the supply chain from producers to final consumers (farmers).

“It allow two (2) stage verification of each agro input in supply chain.”

The First stage of verification will be done by quality controllers, the second stage of verification will be done by the farmer

“Say NO to Counterfeit AGRO INPUTS, Increase your agricultural production”

With 2 stage verification, the system enables you (farmer) to verify that seed that you bought is GENUINE and safe for use.

Our Services

Verification, Feedback provision, and Management of Agro Inputs. Quality guaranteed!

Verification of Agro Inputs

We offer two (2) stage verification of each agro input (Seeds, fertilizers etc.) in supply chain.
The First stage will be done by quality controllers, and the later stage be done by the farmer

Management of Agro-Inputs

This will enable agro input manufacturers manage their prducts, Enabling tracking of products as they moves from production to agents, retailers and finally farmers

Agro Input Feedback Portal

Once the agro inputs are consumed, its vital to get feedback from the farmers. Here we provide the platform.
